STOCKS ON THE MOVE

Important daily list in MarketSmith India that highlights stocks that had the greatest percentage increase in volume above their normal past daily trading level. Usually a sign that institutions are buying or selling stock.

Up In Price: Top stocks which are being BOUGHT heavily by institutional investors. EPS & RS Rank must be a minimum of 70 and A/D Rating must be D+ or better. Stocks must have daily price change of 1/2 point or more with a volume change of 1/2 point or more as compared to 50 day average volume.

Down In Price: Top stocks which are being SOLD heavily by institutional investors. Stocks must have daily price change of -1/2 point or more with a volume change of 1/2 point or more as compared to 50 day average volume.
